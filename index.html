Believer - Imagine Dragons

"Believer"is a song by American pop rock band Imagine Dragons. It was released on February 1, 2017, through Interscope Records and Kidinakorner on the band's third studio album,Evolve (2017). The song was written by Dan Reynolds, Wayne Sermon, Ben McKee, Daniel Platzman, Justin Tranter, Jamie marcel and produced by Mattman & Robin. Imagine Dragons' song 'Believer' has struck a chord with audiences, celebrated for its uplifting message about overcoming challenges. Fans see it as a powerful anthem that inspires strength during tough times. The song's catchy, energetic music and meaningful lyrics have earned praise from critics and connected deeply with listeners who relate to its themes of resilience and personal growth. Its ability to resonate on a personal level has made it a favorite among fans, highlighting its universal appeal and emotional impact."Believer" peaked at number 4 on the US Billboard Hot 100.
